About Charles

Hello! My dream of becoming a baker wasn't always a top priority. Early on, I always wanted to serve as a soldier in the US Army. I enlisted into the Army at the age of 20 and became a Combat Engineer. I served 21 years in the Army with two combat deployments to Iraq, one to Afghanistan, a tour in Germany for 2 years and a 15 month tour to South Korea. I have a beautiful family and two gorgeous dogs.

I first started baking when I was younger. My passion for creating goodness for people to share is my drive. Baking with Charles Bakery is a family centric organization that supports the local community.
