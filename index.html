Sweets for the sweet!

Welcome to my bakery! Our vision is to bring only the best sweet treats to you and your loved ones. Inspired by generations of bakers, Baking With Charles is a family owned and operated bakery.

An assortment of sweets will be sure to meet any cravings that you might have. From fresh baked cookies and brownies, to momma's secret apple pie recipe, we aim to put a smile on your face.
